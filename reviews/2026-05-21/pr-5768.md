## Verdict: APPROVE

## Summary
Removes the stale `themeName` entry from the `useMemo` dependency array in `AppWithRedux` and drops the associated `// eslint-disable-next-line react-hooks/exhaustive-deps` suppression. The `themeName` variable was not used inside the memo callback; `currentAppTheme` alone is sufficient since it already captures theme identity changes. The change was tracked as a sub-issue of the broader hooks-cleanup initiative (#5183).

## Findings

### Positive
- Correct fix: `currentAppTheme` is the actual dependency consumed by `createMuiTheme(currentAppTheme)`. Removing both the spurious dep and its suppression comment makes the linting clean without requiring any workaround.
