## Verdict: APPROVE

## Summary
Removes three `// eslint-disable-next-line react-hooks/exhaustive-deps` suppressions in `NumRowsInput.tsx` by adding the missing `options` dependency to two `useMemo` hooks and `dispatch` to a `useEffect`. Adding `options` to `defaultRowsPerPageValue` and `storedCustomValue` memos is semantically correct — both values are derived from `options`. Adding `dispatch` from `useDispatch()` is safe because Redux guarantees a stable reference across renders, preventing unnecessary effect re-runs.

## Findings

### Positive
- All three suppressions removed via correct dependency additions rather than workarounds. The `dispatch` addition is the standard Redux pattern; `options` dep additions ensure memo values stay in sync with prop changes, fixing a potential stale-closure bug where the memos would not recompute on `options` change.
