## Verdict: APPROVE

## Summary

Single-string change in `app.json`: `Open an Issue` goes from `Créer un ticket d'incident` to `Signaler un problème`. The rationale in the PR body is sound — "issue" in a GitHub context is broader than "incident" and `Signaler un problème` is a natural, widely-understood French phrase. The change is safe and purely cosmetic.
