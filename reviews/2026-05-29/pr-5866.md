## Verdict: APPROVE

## Summary
Adds a background goroutine in `createHeadlampHandler` that evicts `oauthRequestMap` entries older than `OidcStateTTL` (10 minutes). The eviction logic is extracted into `evictExpiredOidcStates` for testability, and `OauthConfig` gains a `createdAt` timestamp field. The goroutine correctly respects `ctx.Done()` for clean shutdown. Two commits with correct `backend: oidc:` format.

## Findings

### Positive
- `evictExpiredOidcStates` is extracted as a package-level function so the test calls the exact same production codepath used by the background goroutine — not a mock, not a duplicate.
- Ticker fires at `OidcStateTTL/2` (5 minutes) ensuring entries are evicted within one TTL of expiry, with proper `defer ticker.Stop()` to avoid goroutine leak.
- Goroutine selects on both `ctx.Done()` and `ticker.C` — will cleanly exit when the server shuts down without leaving a dangling goroutine.
- `TestOidcStateMapEviction` inserts one stale entry (`createdAt: now - TTL - 1s`) and one fresh entry, calls `evictExpiredOidcStates` directly, then asserts the stale entry is gone and the fresh entry remains — concise and deterministic.
