## Summary

Correctly fixes a `react-hooks/rules-of-hooks` violation in `AuthVisible` by hoisting `useQuery` and `useEffect` above the early-return guard, gating the query with `enabled: !!item && isValidVerb`, and adding optional chaining on `itemClass?.apiName` / `itemClass?.apiVersion` to avoid a latent null-dereference. The fix is technically sound. However, the commit subject uses a lowercase verb and is missing the required SubArea segment per project convention.
