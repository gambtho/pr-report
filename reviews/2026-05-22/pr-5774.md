## Summary
Fixes the `react-hooks/refs` lint violation in `Chart.tsx` by introducing a `useCallback`-wrapped `getContainerRect` function with an empty dependency array, replacing the direct `containerRef.current` access and its associated `eslint-disable` comment. The pattern correctly leverages ref stability so `getBoundingClientRect()` is always called at invocation time rather than captured stale.
