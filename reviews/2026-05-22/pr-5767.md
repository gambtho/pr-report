## Summary
Removes two `useMemo`+`useState` anti-patterns where `useMemo` with an empty dep array was used as a one-shot initialiser, replacing them with lazy `useState` initialisers. Also adds `dispatch` to the `useEffect` dependency array for `setAppSettings`, removing the `eslint-disable` suppression. The refactored initialisation correctly reads `defaultValue` (the prop) instead of the stale `options` closure.

## Findings

### Positive
- Switching `defaultRowsPerPageValue` from useMemo to lazy useState correctly captures `defaultValue` at mount time — semantically equivalent and idiomatically cleaner.
