## Summary

Replaces two `useMemo(() => value, [])` one-shot initializers with proper lazy `useState(() => value)` patterns, removing two `eslint-disable-next-line react-hooks/exhaustive-deps` suppressions in the process. The refactor is semantically correct: `useMemo` with empty deps is not guaranteed to be stable across React internal decisions, while `useState(() => ...)` is defined as a one-time initializer. The diff is a net reduction of 6 lines.
