## Verdict: APPROVE

## Summary

Adds five Storybook stories for the `DeleteButton` component (`Default`, `WithAfterConfirmCallback`, `MenuButtonStyle`, `PodEvict`, `NoItem`) along with their corresponding storyshot snapshots. The stories use `TestContext` decorators, `getTestDate()` for deterministic timestamps, and a well-structured `createMockItem` helper. Covers both icon-button and menu-item visual states, and the edge case of a missing `item` prop.

## Findings

### Suggestions

- **[LOW]** All five `.storyshot` files are missing a trailing newline (`\ No newline at end of file` in the diff). Most editors and formatters automatically add EOL. Run `prettier` or add a newline to avoid noisy diffs in future storyshot updates.

### Positive

- `createMockItem` correctly mocks `getAuthorization`, `delete`, `getListLink`, and `_class()` — the minimum surface needed to render `DeleteButton` without live API calls.
- `PodEvict` story documents the eviction-vs-deletion distinction for Pod items, useful as living documentation.
