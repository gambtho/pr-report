## Summary

This re-review covers a new push that adds `frontend: config: Protect oidcAutoLogin from partial setConfig` (correct, atomic commit) and fixes the `OIDCAuth` component to clear the `sessionStorage` flag and navigate to the cluster page on full-page redirect flow.
